EIFS Stucco Installation

Complete Exterior Insulation Finishing Systems installed with proper insulation boards, mesh reinforcement, base coat, primer, and finish coat. Our EIFS systems improve energy efficiency, durability, and provide a clean modern exterior appearance.

Moulding Design & Installation

Custom exterior mouldings installed around windows, doors, columns, and architectural features. We focus on sharp edges, symmetry, and precision detailing to enhance curb appeal and elevate the overall appearance of your home.

Exterior Painting

Professional exterior painting services for stucco surfaces. We prepare surfaces properly, repair minor imperfections, and apply durable coatings that protect against moisture, UV exposure, and seasonal temperature changes.

Veneer Stone Installation

Decorative veneer stone installation for exterior walls, entrances, columns, and feature areas. Our work adds texture, strength, and timeless architectural character to both residential and commercial properties.

ACM Panel & Cladding

Professional aluminum composite material (ACM) panel installation for modern residential and commercial exteriors. Clean lines, sleek finishes, and durable cladding systems built to withstand harsh Canadian weather.

Stucco Repair

Repair of cracks, impact damage, water-related deterioration, and aging stucco surfaces. We carefully blend textures and colours to match existing finishes and restore structural integrity without visible patchwork.

Parging

Foundation parging that protects exposed concrete from moisture penetration while improving overall appearance. We apply smooth, durable finishes designed to withstand freeze-thaw cycles and seasonal weather changes.

Materials We Work With

We use trusted, industry-proven materials from leading manufacturers to ensure durability, weather resistance, and long-lasting finishes.
